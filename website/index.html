The problem we're solving

Pharmaceutical companies invest where profit exists. For the billion people suffering from neglected tropical diseases, there is no profit. So there are no treatments. We're changing that with AI.

How it works

Drug repurposing skips Phase I safety trials — cutting years off the path to patients. Our AI automates the discovery step.

1
Scan Literature
Semantic Scholar API searches 200M+ papers to find disease targets and promising drug candidates.
2
Select Candidates
RDKit screens FDA-approved drugs by drug-likeness (Lipinski rules) and known anti-parasitic activity.
3
Dock & Simulate
AutoDock places each drug into the disease protein's binding site. OpenMM runs molecular dynamics to test stability.
4
Score & Rank
Binding free energy calculations rank candidates. Top hits are flagged for experimental validation.
5
Publish Openly
All findings published free. Any researcher, anywhere, can download and act on the results.
